Himanshu Patel transformed the village into a model village with dedicated efforts

He became the Sarpanch of Punsari village in 2006 at the young age of 23 after completing his studies. During his 10 years of tenure as the Sarpanch of Punsari village, he transformed the village into a model village with dedicated efforts. As a result, Punsari has uninterrupted water and electricity supply, proper drainage system and street lights. The entire village is WIFI enabled. The village is protected with the help of CCTV cameras located at various points and has around 140 loudspeakers installed around the village.
